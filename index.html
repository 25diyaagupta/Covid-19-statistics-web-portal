About COVID-19

What is COVID?

COVID-19 is the infectious disease caused by the most recently discovered coronavirus. The new virus and disease were unknown before the outbreak in Wuhan, China, in December 2019.

Corona viruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans ,several corona viruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MLRS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome(SARS) .The most recently discovered coronavirus causes covid-19

COVID Symptoms

Here are six steps to prevent COVID

Wash your hands regularly for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based sanitizer

Always wear a mask when going out or when you are not alone

Avoid close contact with people , maintain atleast 1 meter or 3 feet distance

Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Stay informed by watching news and follow the recommended practices

If you have fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical assistance immediatly
